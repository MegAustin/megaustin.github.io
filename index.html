About

Meg Austin is a Seattle-based pole dancer, aerialist, hand balancer, and contortionist.

After 13 years of gymnastics, Meg immersed herself in the circus arts. Her signature performance style seamlessly melds dynamicism, athleticism, grace, and flexibility.

Meg has performed and competed on stages across the world. She is the 2020, 2021 and 2022 US Pole Dance National Champion and competed in the Pole Sport World Championship in 2021, 2022, and 2023.

When she's not performing, you can find her teaching at Raven Studios Seattle and New Moon Movement Arts.
